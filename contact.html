Frequently Asked Questions

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery takes 3-5 business days depending on your location.

What if I select Mobile Money but don't pay immediately?

Your order will be placed on "Pending Confirmation". We will only ship the item once we verify the payment. If payment isn't received within 24 hours, the order may be cancelled.

Can I change my payment method after ordering?

Please contact us immediately via WhatsApp if you need to switch from COD to Mobile Money or vice versa.
